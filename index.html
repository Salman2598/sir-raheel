I'm Raheel Aslam.

Raheel Aslam, a seasoned professional, served as the Founder & CEO of GoLive Private Limited, showcasing leadership from March 2015 to September 2019. Following this, Raheel contributed significantly to Unilever, holding roles such as Assistant Manager in OOH Media and later as Assistant Manager in Media, focusing on digital strategies and brand-level initiatives. Noteworthy achievements include driving outdoor media strategies for Unilever Pakistan and spearheading cross-departmental digital projects. Currently, Raheel Aslam is channeling expertise into a startup named "Art of Giving", reflecting a commitment to philanthropy and positive societal impact.
